Free and open-source Persian Calendar with Compass/Qibla and Athan, and Afghanistan holidays, Dari, Pashto, Tajiki, Kurdish, Turkish, Arabic, Spanish, French, Chinese, Nepali, Urdu, and Japanese support.

Supports Gregorian and Islamic calendars as main calendar also.

Also, works with TalkBack accessibility feature of Android.

https://github.com/persian-calendar/DroidPersianCalendar

Please note that the permanent date notification bar can easily be disabled from preference. Also, it has an optional Athan player.
