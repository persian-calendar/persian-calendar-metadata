Free and open-source Persian Calendar
* Compass/Qibla and Athan support
* Dari, Kurdish, Azeri, Pashto, Arabic, Gilaki, English, Turkish, Japanese, Spanish, Chinese and French languages support.
* Supports Gregorian and Islamic calendars support.
* Works with TalkBack accessibility feature of Android also.
* An optional Athan player.

https://github.com/persian-calendar/persian-calendar
